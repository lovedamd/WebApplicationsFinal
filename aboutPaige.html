    D&D Characters I've Played

    As previously mentioned I play D&D here on campus, and here is a list of characters I've played during my three years at Miami.

    Vander

    A druid with a strange affinity for fire magic. He has no idea why that is, but being able to summon fire has gotten him out of a lot of tricky situations in the past so he doesn't question it too much. The flaming dog that follows him everywhere and the lapses in memory are a bit more concerning though.

    Widget

    An inventor who is now partially mechanical herself after an incedent regarding one of her machines. Now more mechanical than not, she doesn't let that accident get in the way of research. After her city was overrun by knights from a neighboring kingdom, she has teamed up with a local weapons merchant to get her work out of town so it doesn't fall into evil hands.

    Pabel

    A merchant who DEFINITELY isn't a pirate. Has had a couple run-ins with the law but now is begrudgingly working with the city guard to get his shop back after it got closed down due to not having proper permits. He's a bit of a kleptomaniac and has gathered quite the collection of trinkets from his adventures including, but not limited to, a bent spoon and a chess piece.
